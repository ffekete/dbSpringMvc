start: mvn jetty:run

Usage:
download file: 					http://localhost:8080/downloadForm/file.txt
upload file: 					http://localhost:8080/uploadForm.html
add new client to database: 	http://localhost:8080/newClient.html
list all clients: 				http://localhost:8080/listClients.html
show a specific client: 		http://localhost:8080/showClient.html?id=3
exception handling demo: 		http://localhost:8080/showClient.html?id=4
