usage: mvn jetty:run

Usage:
download: http://localhost:8080/downloadForm/file.txt
upload: http://localhost:8080/uploadForm.html
new client to database: http://localhost:8080/newClient.html
list all clients: http://localhost:8080/listClients.html
show a specific client: http://localhost:8080/showClient.html?id=3
exception handling demo: http://localhost:8080/showClient.html?id=4
